WiFi

Some of the UK's biggest brands rely on Arqiva to provide WiFi services. We are experts in the travel, retail and hospitality sectors.

    Arqiva provides wifi to many large brands and businesses across the UK, supplying our services to a variety of industries such as retail, hospitality and travel.

    Our high security connections make it safe for your customers to use our services and protect your business from any possible risks. We use a 256 Bit SSL encryption on our network which is verified by THAWTE, ensuring security at all times.

    As part of our comprehensive package, your own account manager will be on-hand should you want to upgrade your services with faster wifi or change your tariff, as well as handle any errors which might occur. Most of these issues can be fixed remotely so you don’t have to worry about an engineer!

    Wifi is becoming popular in just about all public places, with more and more people relying on it than ever. Thanks to the increasing demand for wireless internet, we are able to supply fast reliable wifi to virtually any establishment.
