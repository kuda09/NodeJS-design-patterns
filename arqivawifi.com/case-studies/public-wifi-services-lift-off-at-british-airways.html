Public WiFi services lift off at British Airways

We work closely with many major brands, bringing them reliable and secure WiFi solutions specific to their environment.

Providing a robust and resilient free WiFi solution to British Airways

British Airways needed a robust and resilient free WiFi solution to meet their passengers' expectations and a high level of service to support the offering.

How did we address British Airways challenges?

  • We installed a free service in all twenty BA lounges across the UK
  • We developed a system with ease-of-use in mind
  • We optimised the service to accommodate the latest smartphone and hand-held devices for the best possible user experience

What were the benefits to British Airways?

Our state of the art WiFi system was used in order to meet all customers' expectations and give BA's passengers an improved user experience.

Nick Teale, Worldwide Network Manager, British Airways

"Arqiva offers the capability, coverage and dedicated engineering base to meet our needs, so that we deliver the robust and resilient WiFi solution that our customers demand"

Why did British Airways choose us?

In addition to being a specialist in providing services to busy transport hubs, we have the capacity to scale significant volumes of concurrent free-to-user WiFi connections. We are also able to offer a fully managed service with nationwide engineering coverage and dedicated account management.
