Wholesale WiFi

We understand that you want control over a reliable WiFi service seamlessly delivered to your customers. Our wholesale wifi service does exactly this.

Our wholesale WiFi service has been developed with your needs in mind; Your customers remain your customers.

With no retail offering to conflict with your brand, we give you the infrastructure you need to offer your own customers a seamless WiFi experience. We provide the network, but as far as your customers are concerned, the service is all yours.

You own all customer data, including any captured as part of the WiFi sign-up process and you’ll have full visibility of the users on your network and your customers remain your customers. Always.

Seamless experience

Our service is all about giving your customers a unified service that they can tap into quickly and easily. A service that’s supported by the latest technology so that you can be assured of a hassle-free user experience and great performance. All great news for your customers (and your brand).

We have the scale and experience

Our network is growing. And it’s growing fast. With a focus on premium public locations and high footfall areas, our aim is to be the UK’s leading WiFi network. So whether you are a Mobile, Fixed or Virtual Network Operator we can connect as many of your customers as possible. What’s more, because of our great track record in delivering and supporting high availability networks, with us you can be confident that you’re in safe hands.

“We give you the infrastructure you need to offer your own customers a seamless, fully-branded WiFi service.”
