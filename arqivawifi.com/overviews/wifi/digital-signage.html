Multiple applications, endless opportunities

Whatever your market, our digital signage solution provides you with a powerful channel to attract and engage your audience. Inform, inspire, motivate – it enables you to communicate with maximum impact.

Sophisticated and dynamic

Our solution supports multiple formats including, video, audio, HTML, static images and animation. We work with you to develop the most dynamic and compelling content to help you get your message across.

Grab attention, drive return-on-investment

With our digital signage you’ll have the ability to communicate the messages that you want to deliver and change them in an instant. It’s cost effective and enables you to drive return on investment.

Simple to use and customise

Our signage is easy to control and manage, and has a built-in scheduler so you can amend and adapt the content stream quickly and simply – even remotely via the internet. From just one system you can display two content streams simultaneously across multiple screens.

Drive awareness, build revenue

Digital signage acts as a cost-effective marketing medium for your own organisation. But that’s not all: it also attracts media buyers. This gives you the potential to unlock new revenue streams by offering screen space to complementary brands.

Designed to suit you

We offer a range of commercial options including rental plans and outright purchase.
